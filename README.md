### About Me

I am a student at **NPG po KTC** in Botevgrad, Bulgaria, with a strong passion for software engineering. Alongside my formal education, I am actively expanding my knowledge through **SoftUni's Spring courses** to build a solid foundation in modern backend architecture.

My primary focus is on backend development using **Java** and **Spring Boot**. I enjoy building robust REST APIs, optimizing relational databases, and structuring clean, scalable code. While the backend is my comfort zone, I frequently work with **React** and **Tailwind CSS** to create seamless, full-stack applications for my academic, personal, and internship projects. I am constantly looking to learn new design patterns, refine my workflow, and tackle complex real-world logic.
